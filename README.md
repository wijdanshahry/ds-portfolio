# ds-portfolio

This repository contains data science and market analysis projects focused on understanding consumer behavior, market demand, and business trends using real public datasets.

The work emphasizes exploratory data analysis, statistical reasoning, and translating data into clear, actionable insights for decision-making.

## Portfolio Focus
- Consumer spending and demand analysis
- Market and business trend analysis
- Data-driven insight generation for strategy and planning

## Projects. 

### Consumer Spending & Retail Demand Analysis
Analysis of retail sales data to understand how consumer spending patterns evolve over time and what this implies for market demand and business strategy.

*Data source: UK Office for National Statistics (ONS)*


## Tools & Methods
- Python
- Pandas, NumPy
- Data visualization
- Statistical analysis
- Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab

Background in market research and business analysis, currently building a strong foundation in data science and applied analytics.
